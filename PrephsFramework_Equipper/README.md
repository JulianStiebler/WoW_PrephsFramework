## What it offers

* Smoothly integrates into the Classic UI, orientated on the Retail Gear Manager
* **Smartly remembers Runes and Enchants aswell as Item Locations and does take in fact that you will have multiple items of the same kind and not get confused**
* **Features flyout in Character Window to quickly switch singular item slots. The flyout can be turned to a rune-flyout when pressing SHIFT.**
* **Allows one-click bank and unbanking of items.**
* **Basic and rudimentary trigger system to switch Sets on Mounted or DroppedCombat States.**
    *   "MOUNTED": Whenever you mount it equips, will remember your last equipped and when you demount, it switches back. If you happen to be combated it will queue for droppedCombat.
    *   "droppedCombat": For example if you have a parse set for a Naxxramas Boss, you can click it during pull of trash beforehand and dont forget to switch :)
* Features a QuickBar to quickly switch Sets with rudimentary settings. Will highlight sets that have items not bagged.
* The Icons to choose from do learn smartly from what you own but you can also type in your own IDs and all findable in a small and cute IconManager

Stay tuned for more QoL Addons specifically tailored for SoD.
